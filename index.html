About Me

I am currently a second-year master's student at the Institute for Advanced Study, Shenzhen University. My academic journey has been a blend of passion for scientific discovery and a commitment to excellence in research.

My primary research interests lie in the field of computational biology and bioinformatics, where I am exploring the depths of microbial communities, virus diversity, and plant genome annotation. I am particularly fascinated by the application of deep learning and big data analytics in understanding complex biological systems.

Outside of the lab, I enjoy reading, hiking, and staying active in various sports. I am also an avid traveler and love exploring new cultures and cuisines.
